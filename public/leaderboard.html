Scoring Rules

Points are only awarded when a region (10x10 tile block) is fully cleared (all non-mine tiles revealed). No points are given for partial progress.

  • Base region score = number of safe tiles in region × difficulty multiplier (2) × 100
  • Move Quality Modifier:
    • Forced Move (0 quality points): The tile had only one possible state given its neighbors.
    • Deduced Move (1 quality point): Required combining information from 2+ numbered tiles.
    • Probabilistic Move (3 quality points): No guaranteed solution existed; player made a calculated guess.
    Quality score = (deduced + probabilistic moves) / total moves. Final region score is multiplied by (0.5 + quality_score).
  • Abandon Penalty: If you leave a region with more than 20% of safe tiles unrevealed to work on a different region, the first region's potential multiplier is permanently halved.
  • Streak Bonus: Fully clearing regions consecutively without abandoning any builds a streak. Each consecutive clear adds +10% to the region's payout, up to +50%.
